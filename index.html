Who I Am

A life dedicated
to education

Born in the serene hills of Chisapani, Ilam, I grew up with a deep love for learning and community. Today, I serve as a government teacher at Shree Singhabahini Adharbhut School in Arjundhara, Jhapa — a position I have proudly held since 2060 BS.

Teaching, for me, is not merely a profession. It is a calling. Every student who walks through the classroom door carries the potential to change the world, and I consider it my greatest honour to help them discover that potential.

I reside in Budhabare, Jhapa, where I continue to be an active member of the local community, always eager to contribute beyond the walls of the school.
